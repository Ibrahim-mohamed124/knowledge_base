• To complete setting up the Portworx Enterprise licensing and billing in your environment, you need to:
    • You can apply an enterprise license on your cluster using an activation ID for connected clusters or a license file for disconnected clusters.
    • Once applied, the Portworx Enterprise license is locked to a single Portworx cluster via the unique UUID identifier of the cluster.
    • you can also transfer a valid enterprise license between two Portworx clusters.
